    Here is my most recently played game:

  • Rainbow Six Extraction.
  • My favorite character is Doc. I am preferential to healer classes.

    • Check out Rainbow Six Extraction here

    Rainbow Six Extraction is a game where you play as one of a handful of operators, entering an 'Incursion Zone' to complete objectives while avoiding or killing 'Archaeans', this game's NPC enemy. The game is a PvE, meaning that the game is only a co-operative game, not particularly competitive. This PvE aspect draws me, as I love the allure of working together on objectives while fighting an endless horde. This game is very reminiscent of similar co-op PvE games such as 'Plants vs. Zombies', 'Left 4 Dead', and other various, primarily zombie-based, games. My primary gripe with the game is the occasionally janky detection mechanics that the Archaeans have, which causes some problems if you want to fully stealth a mission, like I try to do.
